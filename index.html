cuepoints

Add subtitles and chapters to YouTube videos

Put chapters to a video

You only need to put the url of the YouTube video. Then you will be able to start creating chapters to that video. When you Finish, you can export those chapters.

(f.e. http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cRmbwczTC6E) check here available formats

Instructions

Once you load a video, you can play it forward and backwards with the YouTube controls. Whenever you want to create a cuepoint, click on new cueopoint above the video

At any point you can remove a point, clicking its corresponding red button

If you want to add a cuepoint between other cuepoints, just click on add next
